Detect & Balance Media Bias

BiasDetector is a Chrome extension that uses AI to identify bias in news articles and rewrites them to present balanced viewpoints, helping you get the full story.

Get the Extension See it in Action

Key Features

Powerful tools to help you see beyond media bias

Bias Detection

Automatically identifies political, emotional, and factual bias in news articles using advanced AI analysis.

Balanced Rewriting

Rewrites articles to present multiple perspectives and use neutral language while maintaining the core information.

Context Addition

Adds missing context that provides a more complete understanding of the topic or issue being discussed.

Comparison View

Side-by-side and inline difference views to easily see changes between original and rewritten content.

Source Citation

Provides alternative sources and viewpoints to support the balanced presentation of information.

Bias Scoring

Rates and tracks bias across different publications to help you identify the most balanced news sources.

How It Works

Powered by AI to provide balanced news

1

Article Analysis

When you visit a news article, BiasDetector extracts the content and sends it to our AI-powered backend for analysis.

2

Bias Detection

Our AI identifies different types of bias including political, emotional, framing, source, factual, and omission bias.

3

Content Rewriting

The article is rewritten to present a more balanced viewpoint, adding context where needed and providing alternative perspectives.

4

Presentation

You can view the original article, the rewritten version, or a side-by-side comparison with highlighted differences.

Example Analysis

Original Article

The administration's disastrous economic policies have led to skyrocketing inflation, burdening hardworking Americans. Prices have surged by 7% since last year, the highest increase in decades, proving that these reckless spending programs are failing the American people.

Experts warn that this inflation crisis will continue if the government doesn't reverse course immediately. Meanwhile, the administration continues to downplay concerns and push for more spending that will only make the situation worse.

Rewritten Article

Inflation has risen to 7% since last year, the highest increase in decades, raising concerns about the impact of recent economic policies. This price increase has placed financial pressure on many Americans.

Some economists attribute the inflation to government spending programs, while others point to supply chain disruptions caused by the pandemic and increased consumer demand as the economy recovers. The administration has acknowledged the inflation concerns while arguing that their economic programs are necessary for long-term growth and stability.

Manual Installation

  1. Clone the GitHub repository:
    git clone https://github.com/amarzeus/BiasDetector.git
  2. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/
  3. Enable "Developer Mode" (toggle in the top-right corner)
  4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder
  5. The BiasDetector icon should now appear in your browser toolbar
